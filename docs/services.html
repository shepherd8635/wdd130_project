Services

Wall Bleach Washing

Mildew, Dust, Dirt, General Cleaning

Specially made cleaning products are spread on all walls to shine and renew walls to their original look. The mixture we use has been tested and proven to work well on all kinds of surfaces and never damage or oxidate.

Solar Panel Cleaning

Increases Efficiency and Lifetime of Solar Panels

We carefully clean each solar panel, increasing each one’s effectiveness and lifespan. We use a special mixture to make sure there isn’t any residue left over on the faces of the panel. We use mostly water, vinegar, and small amounts of dawn dish soap to gently coat the face of each panel, removing any buildup or grime.

Pressure Washing

Shines Concrete and Hard Outside Surfaces

most companies will try to pressure wash as much as possible to make you pay more, but we work with you to best tell what should be simply scrubbed and what should be pressure washed. New cement and stained wood are areas you should always be careful with when cleaning, but we will gladly pressure wash driveways and porches carefully to make them look brand new.

Window Cleaning

Clears Windows of all Mildew, Stains, Dirt, or any other Messes

Basic window cleaning comes with any house wash deal, but you can also choose to get a deeper clean including removing rust stains on window-sealings. Special softer brushes are use and windows are all done twice to ensure cleanliness, then rinsed to leave no streaks.

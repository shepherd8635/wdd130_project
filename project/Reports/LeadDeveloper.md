
### How did you manage your development team and comunicate the tasks for your junior devs?

I made a discord channel that had everyone associated with the project in it. I then used this channel to assign work and to communicate with the client. I also used GitHub's issues tool to mark when work was done.

### How did you communicate with your client? Was there any issues?

The client was very cooperative and answered any and all questions I asked him in a timely manner. He did a really good job. Because of this, there were hardly any communication issues with the client.

### How did you handle publishing your clients site and sharing it with the client?

The client was the owner of the GitHub repository and so he set up the website and gave us a link to it, so he was able to see what was happening as it was updated.

### How was your experience with managing your site with GitHub?

I am pretty familiar with how GitHub works, so I was able to manage my work well.
